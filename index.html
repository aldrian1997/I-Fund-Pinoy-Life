How I Fund Pinoy Life Works

You do not need to know tech. You just need to call us. We take care of everything from your story to your donation page.

1

Call Us

Give us a ring at 02 8351 5854. Tell us about your situation, who needs help, why, and how much you need to raise. No forms or tech skills required.

2

We Verify

Our team reviews your cause and confirms your identity. We may ask for documents like hospital bills or barangay certificates to protect donors and build trust.

3

We Build Your Page

We write your fundraiser story, set up your donation page, and connect secure PayPal payments. Your page is usually ready within 24 hours.

4

You Collect

Funds go directly to you through PayPal. We keep your page updated and support you throughout the entire campaign until you reach your goal.

Why choose us

We Do the Hard Work So You Do Not Have To

Starting a fundraiser when you are already going through hardship is overwhelming. That is why we handle everything from start to finish.

  • Personal, Human Service
    You talk to a real person on the phone. We listen to your story and help you share it in a way that connects with donors.
  • Thorough Verification
    Every campaign is checked before it goes live. This builds trust with donors and keeps the platform honest and reliable.
  • Secure PayPal Payments
    Donations are collected through PayPal, one of the most trusted payment platforms in the world. Donors give with confidence.
  • Ongoing Support
    We post updates on your behalf, answer donor questions, and keep the campaign moving from the first day to the last.
