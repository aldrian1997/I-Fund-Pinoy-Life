Aldrian's Story

"My name is Nicyl Loyola. I am Aldrian's mother. He is in the hospital right now with dengue and I cannot afford to pay the bill. I am asking for your help."

Aldrian is my eldest son. He has always been the responsible one in our family. He works hard, he does not complain, and he has always looked out for his younger siblings. That is just who he is.

A few days ago, Aldrian came home with a high fever that would not go away. We brought him to the hospital and the doctors confirmed he has dengue. His platelet count dropped quickly and he needed to be admitted right away. He is being monitored and treated, and we are praying for his recovery every day.

The hospital bill has been growing and I do not have the means to pay it. I work to support our family but what I earn is not enough for something like this. I have already spent what little we had. The hospital is waiting for payment, and I cannot bring my son home until that is settled.

I did not want to ask for help. But I have no other choice right now and I want to do right by my son. He has always worked hard for this family and I want to be there for him the same way.

The goal is $2,000, which covers the full hospital bill including his room, medicines, IV fluids, blood tests, and doctor's fees. Every dollar you give goes directly to bringing Aldrian home.

If you cannot give, please share this page. It costs nothing and it might reach someone who can help. Thank you for reading this. God bless you.

Common Questions

Is this fundraiser verified?
Yes. I Fund Pinoy Life has verified Nicyl Loyola's identity and reviewed the hospital admission documents for Aldrian. This campaign is genuine.
Where does the money go?
All donations go directly to paying Aldrian's hospitalization bill. Funds are released to the family to settle the hospital account. Nothing is kept by I Fund Pinoy Life beyond PayPal's standard processing fee.
Can I donate from outside the Philippines?
Yes. PayPal accepts donations from most countries. The goal is in US dollars and PayPal will handle the currency conversion from your local currency automatically.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes. Check the Anonymous box in the form below and your name will not appear publicly on this page.
Will I get a receipt?
Yes. PayPal sends an automatic confirmation and receipt to your email address immediately after each completed donation.
What if the goal is exceeded?
Any amount raised beyond $2,000 will go toward Aldrian's follow-up medications and recovery needs after discharge. Nothing extra is kept by I Fund Pinoy Life.
